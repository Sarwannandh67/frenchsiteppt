About This Project

This interactive website aims to provide an engaging and educational experience about French history. Through the use of modern design elements, interactive features, and multimedia content, we hope to make learning about the rich history of France more accessible and enjoyable.

The website covers major historical periods from ancient Gaul to modern times, highlighting key events, figures, and movements that shaped the nation. Each topic includes text descriptions, images, and AI-generated videos to provide a comprehensive learning experience.
